Deep in the lush rice fields of Hualien County, Taiwan, the Pangcah (Amis) Indigenous community lives at the intersection of two spiritual worlds.

This 52-minute documentary follows the young shaman Marang Panay and Father Joseph, a Catholic missionary working in the same community.

The film explores the coexistence of shamanic traditions and Christian faith.
